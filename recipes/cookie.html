Cookie

The recipe for cookies

A cookie (American English), or a biscuit (British English), is a baked or cooked snack or dessert that is typically small, flat and sweet. It usually contains flour, sugar, egg, and some type of oil, fat, or butter. It may include other ingredients such as raisins, oats, chocolate chips, nuts, etc.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose baking flour
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Choclate chips
  • Baking Soda

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375F
  2. In a bowl mix sugar and water
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla until smooth. Stir in flour mixture just until blended (dough will be stiff). Stir in chocolate chips and nuts
  4. Onto ungreased cookie sheets, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart
  5. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft). Cool 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack. Cool completely, about 30 minutes. Store covered in airtight container.
